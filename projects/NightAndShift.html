Project Description

Inspired by Five Nights at Freddy's and Willy's Wonderland, Night & Shift thrusts you into a tense horror experience where a single moment of inattention can be fatal. This game is a student project developed over 2 months at the University of Quebec at Chicoutimi (UQAC) using Unreal Engine.

The Story: Your former boss is desperate. His staff quit without a word, the diner was packed all weekend, and he desperately needs someone to clean up the mess. Being the good former employee you are, you agree to help out for a few hours. After all, the pay is good and the job is simple: mop the floors, do the dishes, and take out the trash. But in the dead of night, the diner isn't as empty as it seems. Something is roaming in the dark. Something is watching you.

  • Do Your Job: Complete various cleaning chores through a system of dynamic QTEs. Keep your focus on the task at hand, but always keep an ear out for footsteps.
  • Hide to Survive: You are completely unarmed. If the creature gets too close, your only option is to find a hiding spot and pray it doesn't see you.
  • Survive the Weekend: Make it through Friday, Saturday, and Sunday night. The tension ramps up as the nights progress.
