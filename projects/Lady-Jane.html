Project Description

Lady Jane: Voleuse Extraordinaire is a solo PC game combining infiltration and 3D platforming mechanics[cite: 3]. Set in 1987, the narrative follows Lady Jane Hawthorne, a respected member of English high society who loses her inheritance[cite: 5]. After her family unjustly seizes her late father's art collection [cite: 6], Jane teams up with her friend Jasper Campbell to steal the paintings back[cite: 7]. Because she refuses to use violence, players must rely on intelligence, stealth, and fluid parkour to navigate environments, evade guards, and secure the loot[cite: 8, 9, 70, 71]. The game features a striking "comic-book" art direction that utilizes bold cel-shading, ink lines, and Ben-Day dots to create a highly stylized visual experience[cite: 22, 23, 50, 181].
